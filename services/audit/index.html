Cybersecurity Auditing

Created at: Sep 08, 2021
Reading Time: 4 minutes

As cyber threats become more sophisticated and diverse, companies are facing an increasing need to ensure their cybersecurity posture is strong enough to protect their sensitive data and information.

Cybersecurity auditing is a critical process that helps organizations identify and address potential vulnerabilities and risks to their systems, networks, and applications.

NCRYPTO provides a range of cybersecurity auditing services that can help clients assess and improve their security posture and stay ahead of the evolving threat landscape.

Our Cybersecurity Auditing Process

Our cybersecurity auditing process consists of several steps that are designed to comprehensively evaluate an organization's security controls and identify areas that require improvement. Some of the key steps in our process include:

1. Discovery and Assessment

We start by conducting a discovery and assessment phase to gain an understanding of the client's IT infrastructure, security controls, and policies. We review documentation, interview key stakeholders, and perform an initial scan of the network and systems to identify potential vulnerabilities and weaknesses.

2. Vulnerability Scanning and Penetration Testing

We use advanced tools and techniques to perform vulnerability scanning and penetration testing to identify and exploit potential security weaknesses. We perform both internal and external testing to evaluate the effectiveness of perimeter defenses, identify potential misconfigurations, and evaluate the effectiveness of intrusion detection and prevention systems.

3. Risk Assessment

We conduct a risk assessment to identify and prioritize potential security risks based on their likelihood and potential impact. This helps clients prioritize their security investments and address the most critical risks first.

4. Compliance and Regulatory Assessment

We assess clients' compliance with relevant regulatory requirements and standards such as PCI DSS, HIPAA, and GDPR. We help clients understand their compliance obligations and develop a roadmap for achieving and maintaining compliance.

5. Report and Remediation

We provide clients with a comprehensive report that details our findings, recommendations, and suggested remediation steps. We work closely with clients to prioritize and implement remediation activities and help them improve their security posture.
