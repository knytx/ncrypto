This Cookies Policy explains how NCRYPTO Labs Private Limited ("NCRYPTO", "we", "us", "our") uses cookies and other similar technologies on our website, https://ncrypto.co (the "Website"). By using our Website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this Cookies Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. Cookies are used to improve your user experience by allowing the website to remember your actions or preferences over time. Cookies are also used for analytics and advertising purposes.

What types of cookies do we use?

We use the following types of cookies on our Website:

  1. Strictly necessary cookies: These cookies are essential for our Website to function properly. They enable you to navigate our Website and use its features.
  2. Performance cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our Website. They allow us to see which pages are most popular, how long visitors stay on our Website, and other statistics related to our Website's usage.
  3. Functionality cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and choices, such as your language preference, and personalize your experience on our Website.
  4. Targeting and advertising cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and your interests.

How long do cookies stay on your device?

Cookies can stay on your device for different periods of time, depending on their purpose. Some cookies are session cookies that are deleted when you close your browser, while others are persistent cookies that remain on your device until they expire or you delete them.

How to control cookies?

You can control cookies by changing your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may limit your ability to use some of the features on our Website.

Your consent
